What's New in gobjectogen?

[0.2.0] - 2016-01-02
====================

Add
---

- `gobjectogen`: tool to generate GObject C code/header.
- `gobjectaccessor`: tool to generate GObject accessor code/header.
- `genumogen`: tool to generate GEnum declaration.
